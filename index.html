Cal Hypo vs. The Competition

Calcium Hypochlorite (Cal Hypo) delivers superior performance compared to Trichlor and Dichlor. See the difference:

Feature Cal Hypo Trichlor Dichlor
Available Chlorine 65-78% 90% 56-62%
pH Impact Raises pH (ideal for acidic pools) Lowers pH (requires balancing) Neutral
Cyanuric Acid (CYA) None added (no chlorine lock) Adds CYA (builds up over time) Adds CYA (builds up over time)
Shelf Life 3+ years (highly stable) 1-2 years 1-2 years
Sunlight Stability Excellent (no degradation) Good (with CYA) Good (with CYA)
Cost Efficiency Lower cost per pound of chlorine Higher cost Higher cost
