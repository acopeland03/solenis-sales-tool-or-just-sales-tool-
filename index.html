Cal Hypo: The Clear Winner

When customers compare options, Cal Hypo's advantages become clear. While liquid bleach may appear cheaper upfront, its instability and low concentration make it more expensive over time. Trichlor and dichlor add cyanuric acid that reduces sanitizer effectiveness and requires costly water replacement.

Feature Cal Hypo Liquid Bleach Trichlor Dichlor
Chlorine Strength 65-73% 10-12% 90% 56-62%
Stability Excellent (long shelf life) Poor (degrades quickly) Good Good
CYA Buildup None None Yes (major issue) Yes (moderate issue)
Cost Effectiveness Excellent Poor (high volume needed) Good Moderate
Shock Performance Excellent Poor Moderate Moderate
Storage Easy (dry, compact) Difficult (liquid, corrosive) Easy Easy

Bottom Line

Cal Hypo delivers superior performance without the long-term problems of CYA buildup or the inconvenience of liquid handling. It's the smart choice for dealers and pool owners who want reliable, cost-effective sanitization.
